About Jujutsu Kaisen

Jujutsu Kaisen is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Gege Akutami. It has been serialized in Shueisha's Weekly Shōnen Jump since March 2018.

The story follows Yuji Itadori, a high school student who joins a secret organization of Jujutsu Sorcerers to eliminate a powerful Curse named Ryomen Sukuna, of whom Yuji becomes the host. Jujutsu Kaisen is set in a world where Curses are created from negative human emotions, and it explores themes of life and death through its characters' battles against these supernatural forces.

This gallery is dedicated to showcasing the stunning artwork from both the manga and anime, as well as fan creations inspired by the series.
